About Allen

My name is Allen. I was born in Nigeria but live in Canada. I grew up very drawn to computers and design. Growing up, I would usually take apart our home desktop computer and put it back together before my dad got back from work. While design was my first love, computers and software in general have always been very fascinating to me. Here are a few facts about me:
  1. I love creating designs, either by drawing or computer graphics
  2. I grew to love photography, because it meant I could create real life representations of my imagination.
  3. I became very interested in Front end web technologies as it meant I could make designs interactive.
